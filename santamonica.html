🌊 Santa Monica Guide 🌊

  1. Santa Monica Pier 🎡

    • Has a ferris wheel, arcade games, and food stands.
    • Great place to watch sunsets over the Pacific Ocean.
    • Often featured in movies and TV shows.

  2. Bike Ride to Pacific Palisades 🚲

    • You can rent bikes along the beach for about $10 an hour.
    • Ride north along the coast toward Pacific Palisades.
    • The ride takes about 30 minutes one way.
    • It’s about 30 minutes back, making it a full beach experience.
    • You ride along the ocean with views the entire time.

  3. Surf Lessons 🏄

    • Santa Monica has many beginner friendly surf instructors.
    • Lessons are available for all ages and skill levels.
    • Boards and wetsuits are usually included.

  4. Third Street Promenade 🛍️

    • Outdoor shopping street with stores, food, and entertainment.
    • Street performers often play music or do shows.
    • Popular place for walking and hanging out.
