🛍️ Melrose Avenue Guide 🛍️

  1. Thrifting on Melrose

    • Melrose is one of the best places in LA for thrift shopping.
    • You can find vintage clothing, rare streetwear, and unique outfits.
    • Popular thrift stores include Wasteland and 2nd Street.
    • Many influencers come here to build outfits for Coachella or festivals.

  2. Community Goods ☕

    • Aesthetic cafe known for matcha and minimalist design.
    • Very popular with students, influencers, and creators.
    • Great place to take photos and relax between shopping.
    • Known for its Einspanner drink which is matcha with cold foam and flaky salt on top.

    Image source: Community Goods Cafe, Melrose Avenue

  3. Shopping

    • Mad Happy: Comfortable sweatsets
    • Chrome Hearts: luxury streetwear and jewelry brand
    • Rick Owens store nearby for luxury fashion
    • Melrose is a mix of luxury and street fashion culture

  4. Instagram Spots For Pictures

    • Pink walls and street art murals are everywhere.
    • Perfect location for aesthetic photoshoots.
