🎬 Hollywood Guide 🎬

  1. Griffith Observatory Hike (Fern Dell Trail)

    • You can hike from Fern Dell Trail up to Griffith Observatory.
    • The hike is surrounded by nature and trees the entire way.
    • The Griffith Observatory is a public landmark in Los Angeles.
    • It functions as a planetarium, science museum, and observatory.
    • It offers one of the best views of the city skyline.

    Image source: Griffith Observatory, Los Angeles

  2. Hollywood Sign Hike (Bronson Canyon Trail)

    • This hike starts at Bronson Canyon, near Hollywood.
    • The trail leads uphill toward the Hollywood Sign.
    • It was originally built in 1923 as a real estate advertisement.
    • It sits on Mount Lee at 1,578 feet above sea level.

  3. Beachwood Cafe Stop

    • Located near the Hollywood Hills area.
    • A popular stop after hiking the Hollywood Sign.
    • Known for breakfast, coffee, and really good matcha.
    • Reservations are recommended because it gets busy.
    • It became popular after being mentioned by Harry Styles in one of his songs.
