Beverly Hills Guide

  1. Rodeo Drive 🛍️

    • Home to designer brands like Gucci, Prada, and Louis Vuitton.
    • Perfect place for window shopping and sightseeing.

  2. Erewhon 🥤

    • A trendy upscale grocery store popular with celebrities.
    • Known for expensive smoothies and healthy food options.
    • Their “famous smoothies” are often seen on social media, typically Hailey Bieber's smoothie.
    • Very aesthetic interior and a popular LA influencer spot.

    Image source: Hailey Bieber / Erewhon smoothie (from Erewhon website)

  3. Go Greek Yogurt 🍓

    • A popular frozen yogurt and healthy dessert spot.
    • Located near Beverly Hills shopping areas, next to Jon and Vinny's.
    • Quick stop for a sweet treat while walking around and open late.

  4. Celebrity Homes and Streets 🚗

    • Many tours drive through Beverly Hills neighborhoods.
    • You can see luxury mansions and gated estates of famous people.
    • Palm tree streets for aesthetic photos.
